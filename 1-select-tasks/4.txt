Create a query to return:

| Customer Id | Total number of Orders | % of all orders |
 
order data by % - higher percent at the top.

DECLARE @TotalNumberOfAllOrders DECIMAL
SELECT @TotalNumberOfAllOrders = COUNT(*) FROM Northwind.dbo.Orders
SELECT CustomerID, COUNT(*) AS TotalNumberOfOrders, (COUNT(*)/@TotalNumberOfAllOrders*100) AS '% of all orders'
FROM Northwind.dbo.Orders
GROUP BY CustomerID
