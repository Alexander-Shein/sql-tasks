Create a query to return:

| Customer Id | Total number of Orders | % of all orders |
 
order data by % - higher percent at the top.

MAKSIM SARVAS:'SELECT [o].[CustomerID], COUNT([o].[CustomerID]) AS [Total number of Orders],COUNT([o].[CustomerID])/COUNT([o].[OrderID])*100 AS [% of all orders] 
FROM [dbo].[Orders] [o] 
 GROUP BY[o].[CustomerID] 

 ORDER BY COUNT([o].[CustomerID]) DESC;' //НЕРЕШЕНО
