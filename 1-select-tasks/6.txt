Create a query to return all products with category and supplier company names:

| ProductName | CategoryName | SupplierCompanyName |
___________

SELECT [prt].[ProductName], [cat].[CategoryName], [spl].[CompanyName] AS [SupplierCompanyName]
FROM [Northwind].[dbo].[Products] [prt]
JOIN [Northwind].[dbo].[Suppliers] [spl]
ON [spl].[SupplierID] = [prt].[SupplierID]
JOIN [Northwind].[dbo].[Categories] [cat] 
ON [prt].[CategoryID] = [cat].[CategoryID]
