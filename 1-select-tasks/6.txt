Create a query to return all products with category and supplier company names:

| ProductName | CategoryName | SupplierCompanyName |

SELECT [p].[ProductName], [c].[CategoryName], [s].[CompanyName] AS 'SupplierCompanyName'  FROM [Products] [p], [Suppliers] [s], [Categories] [c] WHERE [p].[CategoryID] = [c].[CategoryID] AND [p].[SupplierID] = [s].[SupplierID];
