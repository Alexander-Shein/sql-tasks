Write a SQL statement to get the maximum purchase amount of all the orders.

| Order Id | Maximum Purchase Amount, $ |
_______________

SELECT TOP(1) [ods].[OrderID], SUM([odsDet].[UnitPrice] * [odsDet].[Quantity] - ([odsDet].[UnitPrice] * [odsDet].[Quantity]) * [odsDet].[Discount]) AS [Maximum Purchase Amount, $]
FROM [Northwind].[dbo].[Orders] [ods]
JOIN [Northwind].[dbo].[Order Details] [odsDet]
ON [odsDet].[OrderID] = [ods].[OrderID]
GROUP BY [ods].[OrderID]
ORDER BY [Maximum Purchase Amount, $] DESC
