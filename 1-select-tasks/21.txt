Write a SQL statement to get the maximum purchase amount of all the orders.

| Order Id | Maximum Purchase Amount, $ |

SELECT 
	TOP(1) *
FROM
	(SELECT [OD].[OrderID], SUM([OD].[Quantity]*[OD].[UnitPrice]) AS 'Maximum Purchase Amount, $ ' FROM [dbo].[Order Details] [OD] GROUP BY [OD].[OrderID]) [o]
ORDER BY [o].[Maximum Purchase Amount, $ ] DESC
