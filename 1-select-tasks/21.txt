Write a SQL statement to get the maximum purchase amount of all the orders.

| Order Id | Maximum Purchase Amount, $ |

USE Northwind
;
SELECT TOP 1 ord.OrderID, SUM(ordDet.Quantity*ordDet.UnitPrice) AS [Maximum Purchase Amount, $]
FROM Orders ord
JOIN [Order Details] ordDet On ord.OrderID = ordDet.OrderID
GROUP BY ord.OrderID
ORDER BY SUM(ordDet.Quantity*ordDet.UnitPrice) DESC
