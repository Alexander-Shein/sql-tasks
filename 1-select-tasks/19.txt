Create a SQL query to display customer details whose total orders amount is more than 10000$:

| CustomerId | CompanyName | TotalOrdersAmount, $ |


SELECT [c].[CustomerID] AS [CustomerID], SUM([Order Details].[UnitPrice]*[Order Details].[Quantity]) AS [TotalOrdersAmount, $]
FROM [dbo].[Customers] [c]
JOIN [dbo].[Orders]  ON [c].[CustomerID] = [Orders].[CustomerID] 
JOIN [dbo].[Order Details]  ON [Orders].[OrderID] = [Order Details].[OrderID]
GROUP BY [c].[CustomerID]
HAVING SUM([Order Details].[UnitPrice]*[Order Details].[Quantity]) > 10000;
