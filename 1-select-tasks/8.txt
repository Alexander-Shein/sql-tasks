Create a query to return:

| CategoryName | TotalNumberOfProducts |
____________

SELECT [cat].[CategoryName], COUNT([cat].[CategoryName]) AS [TotalNumberOfProducts]
FROM [Northwind].[dbo].[Categories] [cat]
JOIN [Northwind].[dbo].[Products] [pdt] ON [pdt].[CategoryID] = [cat].[CategoryID]
GROUP BY [cat].[CategoryName]
