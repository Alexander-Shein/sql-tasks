Create SQL query to display the average price of each categories's products:

| CategoryName | AvgPrice |

--task17
SELECT [Categories].[CategoryName] AS [CategoryName], AVG([p].[UnitPrice]) AS [AvgPrice]
FROM [dbo].[Products] [p]
JOIN [dbo].[Categories] ON [p].[CategoryID] = [Categories].[CategoryID] 
GROUP BY [Categories].[CategoryName];
