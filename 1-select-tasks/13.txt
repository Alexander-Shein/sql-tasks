Create a SQL query to count current and discontinued products:

| TotalOfCurrentProducts | TotalOfDiscontinuedProducts |

QUERY:

SELECT COUNT(*) - SUM(CAST([p].[Discontinued] AS INT)) AS [TotalOfCurrentProducts], 
SUM(CAST([p].[Discontinued] AS INT)) AS [TotalOfDiscontinuedProducts]  
FROM [dbo].[Products] [p];
